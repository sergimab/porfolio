Components of an electrical substation

A transformer substation is made up of different equipment and elements that work together to distribute electrical energy safely. Below, we present its main components.

1 Gantry for high-voltage line input 2 Line voltage transformer 3 Isolation and line earth switch 4 Circuit breaker 5 Current transformer 6 Busbar isolating switch 7 Main busbar 8 Busbar voltage transformer 9 Control building and cubicles 10 Power transformer 11 Auxiliary services transformer 12 Medium voltage connection
