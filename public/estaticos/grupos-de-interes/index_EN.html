Environment

Close

Hundreds of organisations around the world

How do we create value?

We are fighting climate change and protecting biodiversity

  • CO₂ emissions: 39 g CO₂/kWh2 - almost 83% lower than in 2015 and 75% lower than our European competitors.
  • Biodiversity: Biodiversity Plan, with the aim of generating a net positive impact in the regions where Iberdrola operates by 2030.
  • Circular economy: target of recycling over 90% of blades and panels by 2030.
  • Global leadership in clean energy: 85% of installed capacity is emission-free.
  • Tree conservation, restoration and planting programme: Iberdrola remains committed to achieving zero net deforestation and, since 2020, has promoted the planting of around 8 million trees.
  • Commitment to reducing water consumption.
